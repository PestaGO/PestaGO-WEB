About PestaGO

PestaGO: Mangosteen Leaf Disease Detection

PestaGO is a specialized mobile application designed to identify PESTALOTIOPSIS LEAF BLIGHT in mangosteen leaves using YOLOv8, a state-of-the-art object detection model.

The system uses a YOLOv8 object detection model trained on mangosteen leaf images to identify:

Healthy Normal, disease-free leaves
Infected Leaves showing signs of infection
Disease Specific areas with active disease

How to Use

  1. Select an image source:
    Gallery: Tap to access your device's photo library and select a clear, well-lit image of a mangosteen leaf
    Camera: Tap to activate your device's camera for taking a new photo
  2. Capture a quality image:
    • Position the leaf to fill at least 70% of the frame
    • Ensure natural, even lighting (avoid harsh shadows)
    • Hold the camera steady to avoid blurring
    • Use a neutral background if possible
    • Include both upper and lower leaf surfaces if symptoms appear on both
  3. Process the image:
    • Review your image in the preview area
    • If using camera, tap "Retake" if the image is unclear
    • When satisfied, tap the "Analyze Leaf" button
    • Wait for processing (typically 3-5 seconds)
  4. Interpret results:
    • Green boxes indicate healthy leaf areas
    • Orange boxes indicate infected leaves
    • Red boxes indicate disease parts
    • The system provides detection confidence percentages
    • You can save or share the analysis results
